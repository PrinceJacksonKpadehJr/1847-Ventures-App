Welcome to 1847 Ventures

Empowering farmers, investors, and communities through cocoa

Join our sustainable mission to transform cocoa agriculture through regenerative practices, fair investment opportunities, and inclusive partnerships. Discover how we’re changing the landscape for good.

At 1847 Ventures, we are pioneering a transformative agroforestry platform in Liberia that delivers both economic prosperity and environmental sustainability. Our unique model fuses premium, traceable Grade 1A cocoa exports with high-integrity carbon credits, creating a powerful dual-revenue stream for investors and farmers alike.

Why Cocoa Investment Matters

Long-term value, social impact, and ecological regeneration

Liberia is blessed with rich soils, abundant rainfall, and vast forest potential—yet its cocoa farmers remain undercapitalized and disconnected from high-value markets. We bridge that gap through cooperative-based models that offer farmers better terms, direct access to sustainable inputs, and non-cash credit systems. By doing so, we elevate livelihoods and secure long-term prosperity for cocoa-growing communities.

Agroforestry That Heals: We cultivate cocoa beneath forest canopies to protect biodiversity and reduce deforestation.
Blockchain-Powered Transparency: Every cocoa bean is tracked from farm to container, meeting the strictest ESG and EUDR standards.
Community First: Our clusters support over 4,500 farmers, and we're scaling to reach 10,000+ farmers, backed by tailored support, healthcare access, and training.
Carbon Credits That Count: By integrating reforestation and conservation into our cocoa model, we generate carbon credits that bring added income and climate value.

As global cocoa demand surges—and carbon markets rise—our platform positions Liberia as a new ethical origin for the world’s top chocolate makers. We're securing partnerships with leading brands in Europe, Asia, and the U.S., while building Liberia’s flagship cocoa estate spanning up to 5,000 hectares.

Our leadership includes internationally recognized cocoa agronomists, climate scientists, and economic strategists with over 100 years of combined experience. Together, we bring world-class execution, local trust, and investor-grade discipline to every project we launch.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To unlock Liberia’s potential as a globally competitive cocoa origin through regenerative agroforestry, inclusive value chains, and digital traceability that improves farmer incomes and environmental resilience.

Our Vision

To become West Africa’s most impactful cocoa and carbon enterprise—creating prosperity for farmers, restoring biodiversity, and setting a new benchmark for ethical cocoa investment across the continent.

Empowering Liberia Through Innovation

Our platform connects smallholder cocoa farmers with premium markets and climate-smart tools, empowering local communities through cooperative governance and transparent value chains. With a focus on traceability and high-quality output, we’re reshaping Liberia’s place in the global cocoa economy.

Smart Tech, Smarter Trade

Every pod harvested is tracked via blockchain to meet global ESG and EUDR regulations. This transparency ensures integrity for buyers and fair returns for producers, enabling sustainable growth rooted in data-driven accountability.
