Regenerative Cocoa Platform

Building Liberia's most trusted cocoa and carbon value chain.

1847 Ventures empowers farmers, investors, and communities through traceable Grade 1A cocoa exports, climate-smart agroforestry, and transparent digital operations.

Our model blends premium cocoa production with high-integrity carbon opportunities, creating resilient farmer income and long-term value for global partners.

4,500+ Farmers supported today
10,000+ Farmers in near-term expansion
5,000 ha Planned flagship estate footprint

Our Mission

To unlock Liberia's potential as a globally competitive cocoa origin through regenerative agroforestry, inclusive value chains, and digital traceability that improves farmer incomes and environmental resilience.

Our Vision

To become West Africa's most impactful cocoa and carbon enterprise by creating prosperity for farmers, restoring biodiversity, and setting a new benchmark for ethical cocoa investment.

Why This Model Works

Every cocoa pod we source is backed by cooperative governance, transparent farm-level records, and practical services that strengthen household resilience.

Agroforestry That Heals

Cocoa is cultivated beneath forest canopies to protect biodiversity and reduce deforestation pressure.

Traceability by Design

Digital tracking supports compliance with ESG and EUDR requirements from farm to export.

Community-First Scale

Clusters combine agronomy support, market access, and local capacity building for long-term growth.
